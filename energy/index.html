About me

I am a software developer that specializes in mobile app development. I spent the last 14 years working as a biologist for the state of Florida.

When I wasn’t waist deep in rivers sampling for bugs I was at my desk developing programs in VBA, python, and a suite of ArcGIS programming tools. Outside of work, I’ve used Java, Flutter and Dart to build mobile applications.

I am pursuing a degree in Computing Technology and Software Development from Valencia College. I am currently enrolled full time and on track to graduate in the fall of 2022.
